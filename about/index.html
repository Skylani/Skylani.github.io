About

Hi! I’m Lani. I live in Los Angeles, California.

I graduated from University of California, San Diego with a bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, concentrating on Software Systems.

I have always been passionate about building things. They can be furniture from IKEA or a computer program or an automated task that I set up on my Android phone. I love that feeling of accomplishment when I finish building something. Having enjoyed working on programming assignments from my coursework, I became interested in front-end web development. I’ve learned a lot from online resources like The Odin Project, Code School and Udemy. Check out the projects I’ve made along the way.

When I’m not making stuff for the web, I watch some cat videos, go on a hike, listen to Jazz Rap, and strum my guitar.
