Hi! I’m Lani. I live in Los Angeles, California.

I’ve learned a lot from online resources like The Odin Project, Code School and Udemy. Check out the projects I’ve made along the way.

When I’m not making stuff for the web, I watch some cat videos, go on a hike, listen to Jazz Rap, and strum my guitar.
